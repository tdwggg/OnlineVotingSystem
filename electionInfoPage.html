Election Rules & Guidelines

Voter Eligibility
Must be a registered Filipino citizen, 18 years or older, with valid precinct assignment and verified account.
Vote Secrecy
All votes are completely confidential. No one can access your voting records except through authorized legal processes.
One Vote Per Person
Each registered voter can only cast one vote during the election period. Duplicate voting is prevented through advanced verification.
Voting Period
Voting is available 24/7 from May 15-20, 2024. You can vote at any time that is convenient for you.
Vote Modification
You can change your vote before final submission. Once submitted, votes cannot be modified or withdrawn.
Prohibited Activities
Vote selling, coercion, election fraud, and unauthorized access are strictly prohibited and legally punishable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote online? +
To register, visit the registration page, enter your precinct number and personal information, create a secure password, and verify your email. You'll receive a verification code to complete the registration process.
Is online voting secure and private? +
Yes, all votes are encrypted end-to-end using military-grade encryption. Your vote is completely anonymous and confidential. The system uses advanced security measures to prevent fraud and ensure electoral integrity.
Can I change my vote after submission? +
You can modify your selections before final submission. However, once you click the final "Submit Vote" button, your vote is locked and cannot be changed. Review carefully before submitting.
What if I forget my password? +
Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You'll be guided through a verification process using your email and precinct number. You can then set a new password securely.
Can I vote from outside the Philippines? +
Yes, as long as you are a registered voter and have been issued a precinct number. You can vote from anywhere with an internet connection during the designated voting period.
How are the election results verified? +
Election results are verified through multiple independent audits using blockchain technology. All results are published transparently and can be verified by authorized election observers.
