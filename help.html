Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for voting? +
Visit the registration page, enter your precinct number and personal information. Create a secure password with at least 6 characters. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox. Once verified, your account is ready to use.
What should I do if I forget my password? +
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your precinct number and registered email. We'll send you a reset link. Click the link and create a new password. You can then log in with your new password.
Can I change my vote after submitting? +
You can modify your selections before final submission. However, once you click "Submit Vote", your vote is locked and cannot be changed. Always review your choices carefully before confirming submission.
Is my vote really confidential? +
Yes. We use military-grade encryption and advanced anonymization techniques. Your vote is completely separated from your identity. No one, not even administrators, can determine how you voted.
What browsers are supported? +
We support all modern browsers: Chrome (version 90+), Firefox (88+), Safari (14+), and Edge (90+). We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best security and performance.
Can I vote from my mobile phone? +
Yes! iVote PH is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and computers. Simply log in on any device with an internet connection.
What if I experience internet connection issues during voting? +
Your progress is automatically saved as you navigate. If you lose connection, log back in and you can resume where you left off. Your selections are safe until you click final submit.
How do I report a security issue? +
If you discover a security vulnerability, please email security@ivoteph.gov.ph with details. Do not share vulnerabilities publicly. We take all security reports seriously.
