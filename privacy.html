Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Data and Voting Privacy

Last Updated: January 2024 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
1 Introduction

iVote PH ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online voting platform and website.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our platform. By accessing and using iVote PH, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information you directly provide and information collected automatically:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and precinct number
  • Account Information: Username, password, security questions, and authentication data
  • Voting Data: Your voting preferences are encrypted and stored separately from your identity
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, device type, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and interactions with our platform
3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To verify your eligibility to vote
  • To process and count your votes securely
  • To maintain and improve our platform
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent voting
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To conduct election audits and verification
  • To send notifications about voting periods and updates
Important: Your vote is completely separated from your identity. We cannot determine who you voted for, ensuring complete voting privacy and anonymity.
4 Data Security & Encryption

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission and at rest
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Secure login process with multiple verification layers
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secured, government-grade servers
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access voter information
  • Regular Audits: Continuous security monitoring and vulnerability assessments
  • Compliance: Adherence to international data protection standards
5 Vote Privacy & Confidentiality

Your vote is your right, and we take vote confidentiality extremely seriously:

  • Your ballot is cryptographically separated from your identity
  • Votes are encrypted with keys that cannot be compromised
  • No administrator can access voter identity linked to their vote
  • Election results are verified through independent audits
  • Blockchain technology ensures vote integrity
  • Vote records cannot be accessed without proper legal authorization
6 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Election Administrators: For vote tabulation and official election purposes
  • Authorized Auditors: For election verification and audit processes
  • Law Enforcement: If required by legal warrant or subpoena
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in platform operations under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When necessary to comply with legal obligations
7 Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant authorities

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@ivoteph.gov.ph

8 Data Retention

Personal Account Data: Kept for as long as you maintain your account

Voting Records: Stored securely for verification and audit purposes, encrypted and anonymized

Usage Logs: Retained for 12 months for security and performance analysis

9 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Prevent fraudulent activity

You can control cookie settings in your browser, but disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

10 Children's Privacy

iVote PH is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

11 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing your information.

12 International Data Transfer

Your information is primarily stored within the Philippines in secure government facilities. If international data transfer occurs, it will be in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

13 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of iVote PH constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
